About Visionary Sounds

Visionary Sounds is a house music record label founded by Tremor Rsa (Maninjwa Ntsikayomzi Ngqibeko) in South Africa. We are dedicated to releasing authentic, emotive house music that resonates with listeners worldwide.

Our mission is to discover and nurture underground talent, pushing the boundaries of melodic and Afro-infused house. Every release is curated with a focus on hypnotic rhythms, warm basslines, and storytelling.

Based in South Africa and operating globally, Visionary Sounds has become a home for artists who seek to express raw emotion through electronic music. We believe in community, vinyl culture, and the power of the dancefloor.

Join us on this sonic journey — whether you're a producer, a DJ, or a lover of house music, you belong here.
